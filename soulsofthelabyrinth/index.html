Souls of the Labyrinth




History

The world of Souls of the Labyrinth is formed by a huge labyrinth created as a gift from light to darkness, which after years of living with humans out of sheer anger and envy created grotesque monsters thus beginning a war known as endless war.

As a form of penance light and darkness removed the senses from the labyrinth and invoked a curse that would transform all those who challenged the center of the labyrinth into stone.

The story begins when Soffia and her parents in search of a better place to live end up getting lost and absorbed by the curse and with this mistake turned into stone. Soffia then goes in search of the center of the labyrinth believing in old stories told by the elders.
