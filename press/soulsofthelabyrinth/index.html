Description

Souls of the labyrinth is an epic game about a 13-year-old girl in a giant maze against enemies twice as big as her, having to defeat them to finish a curse that took her parents, inspired by Shadow of the Colossus, Legend of Zelda and Titan souls.

History

The light was born and with the light the darkness was formed.

With jealousy of the grandeur of light the darkness created the walls of the labyrinth and everyone was happy

The labyrinth feeling cold asked the earth to embrace and with that the mountains were created. In their heated warmth of joy the first lives were formed, plants and trees turned to life. But the maze still felt very lonely, so he created humans.

They were smart and cheerful as well as very curious, he then gave a single rule to humans, that they never climb their walls.

But their curiosity was stronger as his laziness, then the rule was broken human found himself greater than the labyrinth above it.

The labyrinth was enraged by this act, and with his feelings beasts were created with horns and fangs, smart as well, but not so curious and more loyal to the rules of the labyrinth, the minotaurs were loved by the labyrinth.

Humans could not stand the love of the labyrinth for something so horrendous then the "War of Doom" began. Humans were weaker than the minotaurs, but their intelligence was greater and their willpower.

Near the end, with the humans losing the oldest humans remembered the stone lay behind the mountains, skin of the labyrinth, strong and malleable, stronger than anything in that world.

Then the humans annihilated their enemies and with the blood of their enemies received the curse, all those who are ambitious will become stone. And the time of the humans is gone.

Features

  • Fight enemies twice as big as you
  • Engaging difficulty level
  • A beautiful open world full of energy and secrets
  • Two different endings for you to discover
