Description

A small independent studio of games with collaborators in several states of Brazil, focused on producing games in 3D environments that bring a feeling to the player,founded in 2016.

History

Moonrise studio came up as a fresh start, that decision to do differently than it takes after returning home at night, the feeling of seeing the moon rise once more in the sky.

An independent studio from Brazil, with people from every corner working together in short games. Founded in the middle of 2016 we participated in some gamejams and now we are producing our first game.

We are focused on establishing our place not only in the Brazilian game scene but also global, creating a good fan base and collaborators, we work the feeling of the game to give the best for our players.

Every game is a new world, every game is a fresh start, every game is a moonrise.
